REVIVE

2018 Fall

Videographer
Visual Effect
IAT 344 Movind Images in SIAT. This course required us to learn the fundamentals of digital video production, including camera and composition skills, the role of sound, lighting, and continuity and montage editing.

REVIVE is a documentary with the topic of Metacreation Lab for Creative AI. It introduced how creative AI works with the music, how it has been applied in artistic practices from general to details.

Brainstorm

We aim to show how creative AI is applied in artistic practices, what is visualized music looks like, and the processes about create super-human musicians with artificial intelligence. Considering this topic is about technology, we would create a sense of high-tech and aesthetics to audience.

Post-Production

During the process of post-production, we decided the make red and black as our main color considering people with color blindness and the footage style as well. To increase sense of high-tech and futuristic, we have applied VFX a lot, including combining the character with visualized sound image together.
