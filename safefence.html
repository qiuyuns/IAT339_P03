SAFE FENCE

2018 Summer

UI
UX
Illustrator
IAT 334 Interface Design in SIAT. I've got to know the art and design of human-computer interfaces, design methods, prototyping and evaluation of user interfaces. In the working process, the whole team need to examine issues of interactivity and its relation to human contexts and technological systems.

Safe Fence is a parental control and child safety mobile app that brings families the security by enabling them to set up safe areas for their children. Safe Fence remains the connections between you and your kids while still respects their privacy.

Brainstorm

We would like to explore a relatively new area that has potential to develop. We found geofence is interesting and unique. We then develop the idea to design a friendlier version of parental control application by combining with the idea of geofence.

Refinement

We designed two different versions for parents and children responsively. Each version is designed based on users' needs, parents are able to check their kids safety, kids are able to see their safe areas clearly.
